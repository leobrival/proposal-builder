| Stage | % of Content | Content Types | Goal |

### 1.5 Content Mix Formula

**Weekly Content Mix (Rule of Thirds adapted):**

- **40% Educational**: How-tos, tutorials, tips (ToFu)
- **25% Engaging**: Stories, case studies, behind-the-scenes (MoFu)
- **20% Promotional**: Product features, testimonials (BoFu)
- **15% Community**: Polls, Q&A, user spotlights

**Daily Rhythm:**

| Day | Content Theme | Platform Focus | Content Type |
|-----|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Monday | Motivation / Week start | LinkedIn, Twitter | Inspirational + tip |
| Tuesday | Educational deep-dive | Blog, LinkedIn | Tutorial, how-to |
| Wednesday | Engagement / Community | Twitter, Discord | Polls, Q&A, UGC |
| Thursday | Case study / Social proof | LinkedIn, Blog | Success story |
| Friday | Light / Weekend content | Twitter, Instagram | Tips roundup, fun facts |
| Saturday | Behind-the-scenes | Twitter | Building in public |
| Sunday | Reflection / Prep | Email (optional) | Week preview |

### 1.6 Brand Voice Application

From brand-package.md, apply these voice attributes to content:

| Attribute | Content Application |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Confident** | "Create proposals that get brands to say yes" (not "try to create") |
| **Supportive** | "You've got this. Your first proposal is 5 minutes away." |
| **Clear** | Simple language, 7th-grade reading level, no jargon |
| **Empowering** | Focus on user capability, not product dependency |
| **Warm** | Human tone, celebrate wins, acknowledge frustrations |
